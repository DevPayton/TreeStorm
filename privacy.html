Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TreeStorm Leads ("we," "us") provides storm-damage alert notifications to tree service professionals who subscribe to our service. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you subscribe, we collect the information you provide: your name or company name, email address, phone number, and the geographic service area (counties) you wish to monitor. Payment is processed securely by our payment processor (Stripe); we do not store your full card details.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide the service you signed up for: monitoring weather alerts in your selected service area and sending you SMS and/or email notifications when qualifying storm events occur. We use your phone number only to deliver these alerts and service-related messages.

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number and subscribing, you consent to receive recurring SMS storm-alert notifications at the number you provide. Message frequency varies based on storm activity in your area. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message, or reply HELP for assistance.

We do not share your phone number or opt-in consent with any third parties for their marketing purposes. Mobile information will not be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of personal information described above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (such as our payment processor and SMS/email delivery providers), and only as needed to provide the service. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your information while your subscription is active. If you cancel, we remove your information from active alerting. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@treestormleads.com
