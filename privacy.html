Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TreeStorm Leads ("we," "us") provides storm-damage alert notifications to tree-service professionals. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

When you subscribe, we collect what you provide: your name or company name, email address, phone number, the state you serve, and the ZIP-code territory you select to monitor. Payment is processed securely by our payment processor, Stripe — we never see or store your full card details.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide the service you signed up for: monitoring weather alerts in your selected ZIP codes and sending you SMS and/or email notifications when qualifying storm events occur. We use your phone number only to deliver these alerts and service-related messages. We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

SMS messaging

By providing your phone number and subscribing, you consent to receive recurring SMS storm-alert notifications at the number you provide. Message frequency varies based on storm activity in your area. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message, or reply HELP for assistance.

We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent with any third parties for their marketing purposes. Mobile information will not be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. The categories of personal information described in this policy exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers who help us operate — our payment processor (Stripe) and our SMS and email delivery providers — and only as needed to deliver the service. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your information while your subscription is active. If you cancel, we remove your information from active alerting and release your ZIP codes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us, and we will delete it except where we are required to retain certain records (for example, billing records) by law.

Your choices

You can stop SMS alerts by replying STOP. You can cancel your subscription from your dashboard at any time. You can request a copy of, or deletion of, your data by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@treestormleads.com
