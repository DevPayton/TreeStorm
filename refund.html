Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We keep this simple and honest: you can cancel anytime, you keep what you paid for through the end of your billing period, and we don't lock you into contracts.

Canceling your subscription

You can cancel at any time, directly from your dashboard — no phone calls, no retention scripts, no hoops. The "Cancel subscription" button is right there in your account.

When you cancel, you keep full access and storm alerts through the end of the billing period you've already paid for. At the end of that period, billing stops automatically, alerts stop, and your ZIP codes are released so other contractors can claim them.

Refunds

TreeStorm Leads is a month-to-month subscription billed in advance. Because you retain full access and alerts for the entire period you've paid for, we do not provide refunds or prorated credits for partial months. Canceling stops the next charge; it does not refund the current period.

Exceptions

We want you treated fairly. If you were charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge, or billing that continued after you canceled due to a technical problem on our end — contact us and we will make it right, including issuing a refund where a charge was genuinely mistaken. We also honor any refund rights required by applicable law.

Free to start, easy to leave

We'd rather earn your subscription each month than trap you in one. If the service isn't delivering value for your business, cancel — and if something went wrong, tell us, because we'd like the chance to fix it.

How to reach us

For any billing question, refund request, or help canceling: support@treestormleads.com. We aim to respond within one business day.
